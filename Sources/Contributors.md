## How to Read These Tables

- **Substantive Messages** â€” messages 40+ characters that aren't noise (greetings, one-word reactions). Higher = more raw contribution.
- **Substantive %** â€” ratio of substantive to total messages. Higher = more signal per post. Contributors like SoundsLikeJoe (91%), Iwan Morgan (91%), and Slow Hand (89%) almost never post filler.
- **Avg Length** â€” average character count of substantive messages. Higher = more detailed, long-form contributions. BatMeckley (214), Slow Hand (225), and peterlabberton (211) tend to write the most in-depth posts.
