# Melodyne

## Definition

Pitch correction and audio editing software by Celemony, known for its polyphonic pitch editing capabilities and [[ARA]] integration with DAWs.

## Context

Melodyne is a standard tool in vocal production workflows, offering note-level control over pitch, timing, and formants. Its [[ARA]] integration allows direct editing within the DAW timeline in supported hosts like [[Pro Tools]], [[Logic Pro]], [[Cubase]], and [[Studio One]], eliminating the need for offline processing.
