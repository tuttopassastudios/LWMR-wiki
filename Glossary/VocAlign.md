# VocAlign

## Definition

Audio alignment tool by Synchro Arts that automatically time-aligns one audio track to match another, commonly used for vocal double alignment and ADR sync.

## Context

VocAlign is frequently used in music production to tighten vocal doubles, harmonies, and stacked vocal layers against a lead vocal. In post-production, it is used for ADR synchronization. It is available as an [[ARA]] plugin and AudioSuite processor for [[Pro Tools]].
